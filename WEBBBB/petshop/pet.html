Vaccination

Vaccinations prevent many pet illnesses.

Vaccinations can help avoid costly treatments for diseases that can be prevented.

Vaccinations prevent diseases that can be passed between animals and also from animals to people.

Diseases prevalent in wildlife, such as rabies and distemper, can infect unvaccinated pets.

Heartworm

Prevention

The treatment for a heartworm infestation is unpleasant and comes with significant risks for your pet.

Parasites are creatures that live on or inside another animal, called the host, and depend on it for their survival. Without a host, the parasite will die.

If your pet is considered “indoors-only,” all cats and dogs should be on a heartworm prevention medication.

Prevention

Flea and tick infestations can impact the health of your pet.

Flea and tick prevention option is right for your pet is to consult your veterinarian.

Flea and tick prevention work to keep your pets happy and healthy, but these preventative measure also help keep your home and family safe from a flea or tick infestation
